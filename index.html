Jewelry Care Guide

Cleaning

Use a soft, lint-free cloth to gently polish your jewelry. For deeper cleaning, use mild soap and warm water with a soft brush.

Chemicals

Remove jewelry before applying perfume, hairspray, or lotions. These products can damage metals and dull gemstones.

Water Exposure

Avoid wearing jewelry in pools, hot tubs, or the ocean. Chlorine and salt can be particularly damaging to precious metals.

Storage

Store pieces separately in soft pouches or jewelry boxes to prevent scratching. Keep chains fastened to avoid tangling.
